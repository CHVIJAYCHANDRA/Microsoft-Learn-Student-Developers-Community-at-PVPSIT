Workshops and Meeting Sessions

At microsoft-mlsa-pvpsit, we organize various events on a regular basis. Generally, our events are related to the world of technology and innovation. We collaborate with different communities from all over the world, providing our members with workshops on in-demand skills such as Web Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Cloud Computing. We also conduct meeting sessions to foster networking among students from various fields.

Hackathons and Internship Opportunities

Our community hosts a variety of competitions, including hackathons and coding challenges. Through these events, our members have the opportunity to showcase their skills, collaborate with others, and gain recognition. Many students have secured internships and work experiences through these competitions. We aim to provide impactful learning experiences and open doors to professional growth.

Helping Everyone to Learn and Grow

microsoft-mlsa-pvpsit is dedicated to the growth of each member. We believe that the strength of our community lies in its members, and that’s why we actively support them in their learning journeys. We frequently collaborate with other communities to contribute positively to the tech ecosystem and help them grow along with us.

Exposure to Students and Mentors Around the World

At microsoft-mlsa-pvpsit, students from all over the world join hands with industry professionals and mentors.
